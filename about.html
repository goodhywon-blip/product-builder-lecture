About

Name → Hangul is a simple, privacy-friendly tool that converts English names into natural Hangul spellings. It runs entirely in the browser and stores custom spellings locally.

Project goals

Provide a clean, focused conversion experience with clear explanations, example outputs, and an optional custom dictionary for personal preferences.

Who it is for

English speakers who want readable Hangul for travel, profiles, creative projects, or name tags. It is not an official romanization system.
