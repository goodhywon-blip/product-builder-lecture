About

Hangul Name Studio is a focused tool that converts English names into natural Hangul spellings. Community suggestions are reviewed before becoming official, keeping results accurate and consistent.

Project goals

Provide a clean, focused conversion experience with transparent review, simple navigation, and high-quality results that respect Korean reading patterns.

Who it is for

English speakers who want readable Hangul for travel, profiles, creative projects, or name tags. It is not an official romanization system.

Quality & transparency

We maintain clear navigation and original content focused on the name conversion experience. Ads, if shown, are secondary to the main content.
