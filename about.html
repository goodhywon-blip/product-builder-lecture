About Name → Hangul

Name → Hangul is a lightweight web tool that helps English speakers render their names in natural-looking Hangul spellings. It runs entirely in the browser and does not send your input to any server.

Home Privacy Terms Resources Contact

Project goals

Provide a clear, focused conversion experience with helpful context (how it works, examples, and the ability to save preferred spellings). The site avoids clutter and emphasizes practical use.

Who it is for

English speakers who want a readable Hangul spelling for travel, social profiles, creative projects, or name tags. The tool is not an official romanization system; it is a practical helper.
