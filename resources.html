Quick notes for choosing a readable spelling.

Choosing a spelling

  • Pick what reads naturally to a Korean speaker.
  • Shorter spellings often look cleaner on name tags.

Common patterns

  • Long “a” often maps to “에이”.
  • “th” varies between ㅅ and ㅌ by preference.
